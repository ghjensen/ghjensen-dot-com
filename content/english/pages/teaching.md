## Facilitation experience:

### Focus Group moderator

- In my research and community-building roles both with the Digital Research Alliance and the Electronic Textual Cultures Lab, I have led 40+ focus group sessions on advanced research topics related to the [Canadian Research Data Platform](https://www.alliancecan.ca/en/initiatives/canadian-research-data-platform) and the [Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS) Commons](https://hsscommons.ca). These sessions have featured researchers, librarians, administrators, and tech experts in Canada and beyond.

### Digital tool and platform demos, workshops, info sessions

- As Assistant Director and Digital Humanities Research Lead in the Electronic Textual Cultures Lab, I led virtual and in-person demos, workshops, info sessions, and discussions to onboard and collaborate with the HSS Commons users (1,500+) and dozens of [research partners](https://hsscommons.ca/en/about/partners).

### Client consultations, demos, and training

- As Executive Director of Media Manifesto, a web design company I founded and operated from 2001-2011, I regularly led meetings, demos, and training sessions with 50+ clients. This work often involved educating clients, empowering them both to understand the services I offered and to responsibly maintain their websites and digital assets on an ongoing basis.
